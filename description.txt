This mod aims to create the inverse or opposite of many themed items and enemies in the game.
So far it adds:
/ Items:
- Angelite Ore and Bar - Inverse of Demonite
- Dark Destroyer - Inverse of Light's Bane
- Asamarum - Inverse of Muramasa
- Sky Sword - Inverse of Blade of Grass
- Storm - Inverse of Volcano
- Day's Frontier - Inverse of Night's Edge
- Death Shard - Inverse of Life Crystal
- Mana Sap Shard - Inverse of Mana Crystal
- Musket Ball..? - Inverse of the Musket Ball I guess?
- Shortswords Galore - Inverses of various things that didn't have a shortsword or a theme per se
- Yard Stick - Inverse of Ruler
- Zombie Hand - Inverse of Zombie Arm
- Swarmer Wing - Inverse of Mandible Blade
- Gloved Blade - Inverse of Bladed Glove
- Fly Snack - Inverse of Flymeal (not in tmodloader yet but whatever)
- Lava Tome - Inverse of Water Bolt
/ NPCs:
// Bosses:
- Peasant Slime - Inverse of King Slime
// Town NPCs:
- Troll - Inverse of Guide
// Enemies:
- Little Shellby - Inverse of Giant Shelly
